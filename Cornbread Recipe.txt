Cornbread Recipe

Ingredients:
aluminum pie tin
1 cup corn meal
3 tsp baking powder
2 cup flour
1 egg
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup shortening
1 cup milk
Notes:
side dish
Instructions:
Heat dutch oven to 350 degrees for Baking.
Combine dry ingredients.
Mix in shortening and egg.
Mix in small amounts of milk until it becomes a batter.
Pour into pie tin.
Bake about 20 minutes, until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
